A comprehensive restaurant point of sale system to manage orders, menu, inventory, and sales directly from your WordPress dashboard.

== Description ==

Obydullah Restaurant POS Lite is a complete restaurant management solution that helps you streamline your restaurant operations. Manage your menu, process orders, track inventory, handle customers, and generate sales reports - all from your WordPress admin area.

= Key Features =

* **Point of Sale System** - Complete POS with dine-in, takeaway, and pickup options
* **Menu Management** - Organize products by categories with images and pricing
* **Inventory Tracking** - Real-time stock management with low stock alerts
* **Customer Management** - Customer database with contact information
* **Sales Reporting** - Detailed sales reports with date filtering
* **Accounting** - Track income, expenses, payables, and receivables
* **Order Management** - Process and track orders with cooking instructions
* **Table Management** - Assign tables for dine-in orders
* **Receipt Printing** - Professional invoice and receipt generation

= Perfect For =

* Restaurants and cafes
* Food trucks and stalls
* Coffee shops
* Bakeries and patisseries
* Any food service business

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/obydullah-restaurant-pos-lite` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Go to Restaurant POS in your WordPress admin to set up your menu and start taking orders

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I add menu items? =
Go to Restaurant POS > Products in your WordPress admin to add your menu items and organize them by categories.

= Can I track inventory? =
Yes! The plugin includes complete inventory management with stock tracking and low stock alerts.

= Does it support different order types? =
Yes, it supports dine-in, takeaway, and pickup orders with specific options for each.

= Can I print receipts? =
Yes, the plugin includes professional receipt printing for completed orders.

= Is customer data stored? =
Yes, you can save customer information for repeat orders and better service.
